# Project Description

## Title
TerraMind Rescue Planet — Offline Gemma AI for Disaster Survival and Solar Resilience

## Short subtitle
Offline AI for terrain risk analysis, emergency guidance, and solar-powered survival during disasters.

## Full description

TerraMind Rescue Planet is an offline-ready multimodal AI system built for crisis situations where internet access, electricity, and communication networks may fail. Powered by Gemma, the system analyzes images of dangerous terrain, floods, fires, smoke, damaged infrastructure, snow-covered areas, and solar-powered vehicles or panels.

The goal is simple: help people understand visual danger fast and receive practical safety instructions without relying on cloud connectivity.

A user uploads a photo from a phone or field device. Gemma analyzes the scene, detects possible hazards, estimates the risk level, and generates a structured emergency report. The assistant can also translate the instructions into multiple languages, making it useful for international rescue teams, local communities, travelers, and workers in remote areas.

The project also includes a solar-resilience layer. For PV-powered vehicles or emergency equipment, TerraMind can analyze sunlight exposure, shadow from mountains or buildings, terrain conditions, and possible parking or route choices. This helps users avoid shaded zones and improve solar charging during field operations.

TerraMind Rescue Planet is focused on one professional workflow:

**image → analysis → hazard → instruction → report → translation**

This makes the demo realistic, clear, and useful for disaster resilience, digital equity, and edge AI deployment.
