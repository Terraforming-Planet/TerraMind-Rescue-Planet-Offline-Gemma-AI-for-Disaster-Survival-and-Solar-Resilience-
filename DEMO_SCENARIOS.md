# Demo Scenarios

## Scenario 1 — Flooded Street

**Input:** Photo of a flooded road, valley, or urban street.  
**AI output:** High water risk, possible hidden electrical danger, blocked route, recommendation to move to higher ground.

**Example result:**
- Danger level: High
- Main hazard: Flooded access route
- Action: Do not enter water, avoid low terrain, move uphill, report location.

---

## Scenario 2 — Damaged Cable or Power Pole

**Input:** Photo of a damaged electrical cable, pole, grid box, or installation.  
**AI output:** Electrical danger warning, safe distance instruction, emergency report.

**Example result:**
- Danger level: Critical
- Main hazard: Possible live electrical cable
- Action: Keep distance, do not touch water or metal objects nearby, call emergency services.

---

## Scenario 3 — Smoke or Fire

**Input:** Photo of smoke, fire, burned area, or forest edge.  
**AI output:** Fire/smoke risk, evacuation guidance, report for responders.

**Example result:**
- Danger level: High
- Main hazard: Smoke exposure and fire spread
- Action: Move away from smoke, cover mouth and nose, evacuate against wind direction when safe.

---

## Scenario 4 — Solar Vehicle / PV Parking

**Input:** Photo of a PV-covered vehicle, solar parking area, mountain valley, or shaded terrain.  
**AI output:** Solar exposure score, shadow risk, suggested parking direction.

**Example result:**
- Solar score: 82/100
- Main issue: Mountain shadow expected on left side
- Action: Move vehicle to open area with south-facing exposure and avoid tree shadow.
